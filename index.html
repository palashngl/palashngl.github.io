Welcome to my home page. I am glad you have found your way to this site and appreciate your taking the time to visit. I received my Ph.D from Security Engineering Lab , Sejong University, South Korea advised by Professor Young-Gab Kim. Previously, I received my Master's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from YMCA, India. Additionally, I have completed a Master of Science in Information Technology from K.J.Somaiya , India and a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology from SNGC , India.

I am currently working as a Research Professor at SEL, Sejong University, Seoul, South Korea. Prior to this, I worked as an Postdoctoral Research at Sejong Uni. and Assistant Professor at K. J. Somaiya College, Mumbai, India, and additionally, I have qualified NET exam for Assistant Professor. My research focuses on the field of Video Synopsis, Unmanned Aerial Vehicle, Panoramic Video Stitching in Autonomous Vehicle, Abnormal Object Detection and Tracking, Human-Computer Interaction, Sensor Fusion and Robotics.
